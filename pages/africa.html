SOUTH AFRICA


South Africa is a country on the southernmost tip of the African continent,
marked by several distinct ecosystems. Inland safari destination Kruger National Park covers vast shrublands populated by big game;
the Western Cape encompasses lush winelands around Stellenbosch and Paarl, wild beaches,
craggy cliffs at the Cape of Good Hope, forest and lagoons along the Garden Route,
and the city of Cape Town, beneath flat-topped Table Mountain.
