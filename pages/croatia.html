CROATIA



Croatia is an Eastern European country with long coastlines on the Adriatic Sea,
encompassing more than a thousand islands, crossed by the Dinaric Alps and dotted with castles.
ts inland capital, Zagreb, is distinguished by its medieval Gornji Grad (Upper Town) and diverse museums.
The major coastal city Dubrovnik has massive 16th-century walls encircling an Old Town with Gothic and Renaissance buildings.



REASONS


Croatia, situated on the Adriatic Sea, is one of the most beautiful countries in Europe.
With its unique location, deep cultural roots, and incredible history it's a must-see.
The Croatian coast is absolutely astounding. There's no shortage of beaches to take your breath away.
The water is crystal clear and turquoise blue. And Croatia has thousands of islands and islets.
Several Stone Age sites have been discovered all over the country along with numerous caves containing hand made object
from people alive centuries ago. Most towns have historical centers with typical Croatian architecture, a must-see.
